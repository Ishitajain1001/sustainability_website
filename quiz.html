YOUR CARBON FOOTPRINT:

If your carbon footprint score was below 60, good job! Your impact on the environment is minimal. Keep up the good work! If your carbon footprint score was above 60, your impact on the environment is a little damaging! You might want to look for some ways that you can reduce your effects on the environment :)

The total amount of greenhouse gases (such as carbon dioxide and methane) produced by human actions is known as a carbon footprint. The United States has one of the biggest carbon footprint averages in the world, at 16 tons per person. The average carbon footprint across the globe is closer to 4 tons.

Individual carbon footprint reduction from 16 tons to 2 tons takes time! By making minor adjustments to our behavior, such as cutting back on our meat consumption, skipping fewer layovers, and line-drying our clothing, we can start to make a significant difference.
